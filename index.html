Privacy Policy

Last updated · 06 Jul 2026

Your data, protected

Medurun collects only what's needed to dispatch emergency care quickly and safely.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Location (for dispatch), contact details, and the medical profile you provide (blood group, allergies, conditions). Shared with the assigned paramedic only during an active ride.

HOW WE USE IT

To assign the nearest ambulance, guide the driver, process payment, and generate invoices. We never sell your data.

YOUR RIGHTS

Access, correct, or delete your data anytime from Edit Profile, or contact support to close your account.
